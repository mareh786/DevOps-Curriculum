# Recommended Resources (2026 Edition)

Focus on free or low-cost, high-signal resources. Prioritize official docs + proven hands-on content.

### Roadmaps & Overviews
- [roadmap.sh/devops](https://roadmap.sh/devops) – Interactive 2026 DevOps/SRE path (free, exportable)
- [milanm/DevOps-Roadmap](https://github.com/milanm/DevOps-Roadmap) – Step-by-step with free links (PDF available)

- The DevOps Handbook (2nd edition – practical how-to)
